The Leakage Detection System

Gujarat, India

The “Leakage Detection System” is efficient, systematic, and amiable one stop website along with a device which has sensors (fire smoke leakage detector, liquid leakage detector, gas leakage detector). When the detection of the leakage is not done on an early stage there occur some severe destruction to the industries economically along with the health of the workers working there.So considering this loss of companies and their workers we came up with the "Leakage Dtetection System" Project.Our goal is to develop a system that eliminates time delay and prevents major accidents causing destruction on a larger scale in the industries by providing timely response and saves from larger financial loss of the industries.Which we are trying can be prevented through our project The user will be notified with the warning through the website and our device which provides detection of any kind of leakage hence leading to the early detection of the leakage. We include Internet of Thing (IOT) which can be used to produce an automatic notification and response to the scene, so this system provides such facilities.
